Ingredients

  • All purpose flower
  • Cornstarch
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • White Chocolate Chips
  • Macademia Nuts
  • Time and Effort
  • Time and Effort 2

Directions

  1. Mixing flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the rest of the not-dry ingredients together and then add in the dry ingredients.
  3. Cover and chill for 2 hours
  4. Preheat oven to 350 deg F
  5. Making balls from the dough and put into a baking sheet that has parchment paper on it
  6. Bake for about 12 mintues with balls 3 inches apart or until lightly browned on the sides. The centers will look soft.
  7. Let the cookies cool for like 5 min
