Make pasta at home with a few simple ingredients

The assignment is to write a paragraph or two describing the recipe. According to the website, this pasta reciple is simple and doesn't require too many steps.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Sal

Directions

  1. Get a clean work surface
  2. Make a volcano out of the flour
  3. Add in the other ingredients to the center of the volcano
  4. Use a fork to break the eggs
  5. Mix all that stuff together into a ball and knead
  6. Seperate dough into 4 piecs and rest at room temp for 30 min
  7. Run through pasta maker
  8. Fold dough into rectangle
  9. Run through pasta maker a couple more times till its thin
  10. cut and cook the pasta
