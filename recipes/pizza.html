Ingredients

  • Flour
  • salt
  • sugar
  • fast rise yeast
  • water
  • oil

Toppings

  • tomato sauce
  • italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • pepperoni
  • mozzarella cheese
  • montery jack cheese
  • parmesan chese

Directions

  1. Mix first four ingredients listed above
  2. Mix water and oil and add flour to mixture
  3. knead for 2 min on floured surface
  4. Put in a greased bowl
  5. Cover and let rise for 20 mintues
  6. Punch down, place on 12 in greased pan
  7. Pat into a circle
  8. Mixing the first 5 toppings ingredients and spread over crust
  9. put some pepperoni on top of sauce
  10. put on 1/2 the cheese
  11. put on the rest of the pepperoni
  12. put the rest of the cheese on
  13. bake at 400 deg F in oven for about 20 min or until light brown
